Challenges

  • Slow Performance

    The PHP-based application was slow due to large amounts of data from IoT devices.

  • Data Management Issues

    Managing 2 TB of data was challenging, with a daily throughput of 20 GB.

  • Customer Issues

    Poor coding made it hard to identify and fix customer issues quickly.

  • High Maintenance Costs

    Upgrading AWS resources instead of fixing coding issues led to high costs.

  • UI/UX Challenges

    Farmers found it hard to understand the workflow due to frequent new features.

Solutions

  • Performance Optimization

    Transitioned to a hybrid stack and used microservices/serverless technologies to improve performance.

  • Big Data Management

    Managing 2TB of data was challenging, with a daily throughput of 20GB

  • Improved Customer Support

    Enhanced coding practices helped developers resolve customer issue faster.

  • Cost Reduction

    Optimized architecture reduced AWS monthly bills.

  • Enhanced UI/UX

    Made the applications more user-friendly for farmers by simplifying the application workflow.
